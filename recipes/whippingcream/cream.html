Ingredients

  • Heaving Whipping Cream
  • Steel Bowl and Electric Mixer
  • Large plastic bowl filled with ice

Instructions

Method:

  1. Place steel bowl on top of plastic bowl filled with ice (the steel bowl MUST be smaller than the plastic and wait for the steel bowl to get cold
  2. Add Heaving whipping cream to cold steel bowl and start mixing on medium or high with the electric mixer
  3. Mix until stiff peaks form and you have Hwiped Cream! Good Job!
