Soft Boiled Eggs

A soft-boiled egg is an egg that has been cooked in its shell until the whites are set to a custardy texture and the yolks are creamy, with a consistency ranging from runny (uncooked) to jammy half-set

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Water
  • Heat

Instructions

Method:

  1. Boil water and place eggs in boiling water
  2. Turn off heat and cook for 6 minutes
  3. Cool in ice bath until ready to peel and enjoy
